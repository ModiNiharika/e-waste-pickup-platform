# Problem Statement - E-waste Collection And Reward System

---

## What is the problem?

In our daily life,we use mobile phones,laptops,chargers,batteries and etc.After a certain period of use, this devices were not useful.And many people don't know how to dispose them.Some people store them and some throw in regular dustbins . 

The problem is that the E-waste contains many harmful and toxic metals like lead,mercury and etc which contaminates soil and water if not handled properly .And giving this electronics to others also lead to data leakage and may be risk to the users.

With rapid advancement of technology,new devices were replacing very fastly and the old devices.This leads to  accumulation of large amounts of e-waste  and making the environment affected by this.

Another major issue is the lack awareness among the people about the nearby e-waste  collection centers and the effect of E-waste in environment and there is no doorstep pickup system till now.

So to solve this problem we introduce a tool which provides the facility of Doorstep pickup and have verified recyclers and offers rewards such as points , money or coupons for the given e-waste to encourage the people .

## Who is facing it?

**Primary :** Households use phones,chargers,batteries and the people were not aware of the proper disposal methods, so this e-waste will be accumulate in the homes.Some people throw them in the dustbins and some store them at home.Especially hostel students who use more gadgets will have this e-waste as there are no proper disposal centres available for them

**Secondary :** Small offices,companies and educational institutions use computers,laptops,printers,batteries and they replace them frequently.As a result,they generate large quantities of e-waste and there will be data security concerns while disposing the old systems.

**Also Affected :** Due to toxic materials in this e-waste there will be soil and water pollution and there will be an increase in public health risks.

## Why does it matter?

This E-waste causes major impact in some ways : 

-**Environmental Damage :** E-waste consists of many toxic chemicals like lead, mercury, cadmium and etc. So if we do not dispose this chemicals properly it leads to soil pollution, water pollution and air pollution which is very dangerous to the environment.

-**Public Health Risks :** Due to improper e-waste handling, harmful chemicals present in the e-waste will spread and lead to health problems for living things . Especially this affects animals, trees,waste collection workers and nearby communities more.

-**Increasing E-waste every year :** As technology is rapidly increasing people are purchasing new phones and laptops and replacing the old ones and the usage of the electronics is also increasing. So the e-waste quantity is increasing hugely. So if there is no proper system there will be more risk and danger in future.

-**Data Security issues :** In old devices there will be photos, passwords, documents and some personal data which should not be shared to anyone. Due to improper disposal of this gadgets there may be a risk of data leakage . 

Threfore, a proper e-waste management and disposal system is necessary to reduce environmental damage and encourage safe disposal practices.

## What would a solution look like?

A simple tool can be developed where :

1. **Users creates request -** User enters the details of the old phones, laptops, batteries, chargers and other electronic devices and raise pickup request.

2. **Doorstep Pickpu facility -** The system verifies the details and assigns nearby verified recycler and schedules pickup date and time. So users need not to go to e-waste centers. There will be only trusted verifed recyclers which helps in safe disposal,proper recycling and data security .

3. **Reward System -** User will get points , coupons, cashback and rewards for the given e-waste which will motivate the society or users to use this system . 

The goal of the system is to encourage responsible e-waste disposal and reduce environmental damage by making the process simple and accessible
